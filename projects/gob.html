Go-Britain Website


This project is about the research and realisation of a new custommer journey for Go-Britain, a retailer which had multiple locations in the Netherlands.

The assignment included the rebranding of their Storefronts, including their own website. We’ve started this project weeks before the pandemic in 2020 hit, which sadly ment we could not realise the website and 3 of the storefronts.
The market research i’ve done to innovate the website led me to a mobile first design, that included their product photos. 
Creating brand, marketing and product photo guidelines to suit the needs of the user and ease of travel within the webstore was one of the final projects i've worked on that were realised.
