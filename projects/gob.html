Go-Britain Website

This project is about the research and realisation of a new website for Go-Britain, a retailer with multiple locations in the Netherlands

The assignment included the rebranding of their Storefronts, including their own website. We’ve started this project weeks before the pandemic in 2020 hit, which sadly ment we could not realise the website and 3 of the storefronts.

The market research i’ve done to innovate the website led me to a mobile first design, that included their product photos and ease of travel within the webstore.
